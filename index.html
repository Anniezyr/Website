About Me

Hi! I am a Game Designer seeking a full-time position.

I specialize in designing immersive and engaging game levels using both Unreal Engine and Unity. My experience includes level design, environmental storytelling, and optimizing gameplay flow to enhance player engagement.

I recently graduated with an M.S. in Game Development from the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT), where I focused on game mechanics, player experience, and level design principles.

I am passionate about crafting memorable player experiences and always eager to collaborate and grow within the game development industry.
